Our Story

About Lumière

👨🏾‍🍳

Founder & Executive Chef

Chef Osahon Eghosa

Osahon trained in Lagos and London before returning home to Benin City with one conviction: the dishes he grew up on deserved a fine-dining table of their own. Lumière opened in 2014 as a six-table room serving his mother's recipes, refined with classical technique — and has been quietly redefining what Nigerian fine dining can look like ever since.

Mission

To prove that West African cuisine belongs on the same stage as any culinary tradition in the world.

Vision

A Lumière table in every major West African city — each one rooted in its own local market.
